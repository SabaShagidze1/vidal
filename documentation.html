The Goytemirov Braking Device

There are problems in brake mechanisms caused by dry sliding friction, We have developed a braking device that works without dry sliding friction, which can be sealed from the environment and work in oil without overheating.

no break pads, sliding friction is replaced with rolling friction in bearings, The breaking device works quietly - without noise, cleanly - without dust, smoothly - without jerks.

longevity

Many times longer service life than a conventional brake mechanism
at least 10 times on large vehichles and 30 times on passenger cars.

Braking Process

The braking device, unlike the traditional one, independently regulates the braking process, and performs the work of the ABC system,
its essence is not to unlock the brakes, but to synchronize it.

The Development

The model of the braking device has been developed, Research work has been carried out, the design of the device has been developed, special design work has been carried out,
tested components and parts of the device was tested and its performance and efficiency were proved.

This braking device can be installed on all vehicles

Without exception and mechanically, without sliding friction, can slow down and maintain the speed of the vehicle, regardless of its weight and the characteristics of rough terrain, without the need to use any additional, often cumbersome units, such as intarder.

Bicycles

For road and racing bicycles, the same type of braking device scheme is used with the preservation of drives. in this case, the braking element connected to the drive - cable, moves the rod, and this movement with the rolling bearing slows down the brake disc installed on the wheel hub.

Motorcycles And Cars

Including racing - high-speed vehicles. The same type of braking device scheme is used, also with the preservation of drives. With our device, there is no need to use an intrader that serves to maintain a reduced speed of vehicles, as well as an ABS system.

Busses And Trucks

A different scheme of the braking device is used, but with the preservation of the service brake drives.
