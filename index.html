The Goytemir Braking Device

We have developed a braking device that works without dry sliding friction, which can be sealed from the environment and work in oil without overheating.

The braking device works quietly - without noise, cleanly - without dust, smoothly - without jerks.

Many times longer service life than a conventional brake mechanism, at least 10 times on large vehicles and 30 times on passenger cars.

This braking device independently regulates the braking process and performs the work of the ABC system.

Old Braking Mechanisms
And Their Numerous Problems

Traditional brake pads are not wear resistant. and the use of wear-resistant pads, for example, made of carbon steel due to the presence of dry sliding friction, will excessively overheat the pads and the pistons associated with them, the brake will then jam, which can cause self-braking and accidents.

Traditional braking mechanisms are either overly expensive or harmful to the enviroment.

New Mechanical Braking Device
Without dry sliding friction and brake pads

a mechanical braking device and at the same time it works without brake pads, pneumatics, dry sliding friction and many other such parts.

Main advantage of our braking device compared to other braking systems is that it's harmless to human health and the environment. We are entering a new era of environmentally friendly, economically profitable and safe vehicles.
